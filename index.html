WHAT DO WE SEE AS DTM COLLECTIBLES?

Over the past few years, we have increasingly linked passion and experience to the automotive industry. While in our opinion the modern car lacks the 'goosebump' factor, many works of art were created in the past in the period of, in our opinion, the pinnacle of mechanization. As DTM Collectibles we try to guarantee this in our collection, with the technical standard of Lusso Classics, our technical specialist. This allows us to guarantee our high standards of quality, originality and reliability. In addition, it is of course important that we have many 'smiles a litre' with customers and our immediate environment of private collectors. Curious? Drop by!
